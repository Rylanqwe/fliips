About Coin Flip Betting

This is a simple coin flip betting website built on the Solana blockchain. Users can connect their Phantom wallets and make bets on the outcome of a coin flip.

Rules

  • Users must connect their Phantom wallets to make bets
  • Bets must be made in SOL
  • Users can bet on the outcome of a coin flip (heads or tails)
  • Winners will receive 2x their bet
